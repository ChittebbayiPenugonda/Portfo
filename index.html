CSSA

What is it? Acronym >> COMPUTER SCIENCE STUDENTS ASSOCIATION What do we do? We host a variety of computer science-related events that you can compete in Do you need experience? Nope! CSSA is fit for beginner, hobbyists, and anyone else with an interest in computer science!
Computer science dominates the world. Everywhere you look, you see electronic devices built from computer architectures, architecture designed using computer calculations, medicine discovered using computer algorithms, and so much more. Put simply, computer science is the key to our future.
Through CSSA, you can participate in many competitions hosting a variety of computer-science related events. Whether you're new to computer science or a seasoned veteran, CSSA is a great place to learn and improve your skills.
With a rapidly-growing community of members, CSSA is a great way to meet and network with other people with a passion for computer science. Join our Official Discord Server to chat with other members!
